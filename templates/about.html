About Stocklerts - A Portfolio Project:

Stocklerts gets its name from the combination of "stock trading" and "news alerts," a project brought to life by Nora Ben Haddou, an ALX Software Developer student. Here's a glimpse into what Stocklerts is all about:

Project Overview:

Stocklertsis more than just a website; it's my portfolio project aimed at tackling the challenge of creating a DIY Terminal for individual traders. The project's core idea is to provide users with a personalized platform, ensuring a tailored experience when accessing stock market information.

Key Features:

User-Customized Platform: Stocklerts allows users to customize their experience, tailoring the platform to their specific trading needs.

API Integration: To ensure that users receive timely and pertinent information for making informed trading decisions, the project integrates two APIs:

  1. Alpha Vantage for market data
  2. News API for relevant news updates

DIY Terminal: Stocklert's approach is to create a DIY Terminal, providing users with a stock trading news alerts application. The goal is to empower users with daily market insights, similar to the functionalities of a Bloomberg Terminal.

Limitations:

It's important to note that Stocklerts does not substitute professional financial advice, stock market predictions, or comprehensive market analysis services. The project refrains from providing buy/sell recommendations to users.
